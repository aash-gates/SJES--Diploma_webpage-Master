    Electrical & Electronics Engineering

    Electrical engineering includes design, develop and maintain electrical control systems and/or components to required specifications, focusing on economy, safety, reliability, quality and sustainability.

    Electronics & Communication Engineering

    Electronics & Communication has been a key and integral part of the technological revolution in the last two decades.
    While Electronics aims to make life simpler and more comfortable, Communication brings them together.
    The tremendous technological strides achieved and the vast application of electronics in our day to day life makes this branch of study all the more interesting and a potential career option.
